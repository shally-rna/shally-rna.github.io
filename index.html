Namtoko Investment CC is a trusted automotive repair and maintenance company located at Unit 16 & 17, Ondangwa Industrial Park, Namibia. We specialise in providing reliable, high-quality vehicle repair and servicing solutions for individuals, businesses, and fleet operators.
